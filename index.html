Paper Published at ICAIN 2025

October 2025

Published the research paper, “Urban Ecosystem Revolution: Integrating Nature's Wisdom with Automation for Sustainable City Living” at the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Networks (ICAIN 2025), held at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus in association with IIIT Allahabad. An extension of Project F.O.R.E.S.T., the paper proposes a model to improve urban well-being by creating automated green spaces using Arduino, smart sensors, and piezoelectric energy.
